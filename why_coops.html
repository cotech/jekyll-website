Why co-ops?

Co-operatives are businesses in many sectors and in many sizes, but with one crucial difference: they are owned and democratically controlled by their members. That means people in the business, not external shareholders, set the direction and share the value.

CoTech coops are worker-owned co-operatives, meaning that the members are the workers. So the people delivering the projects co-own and control the business together.

Cooperatives around the world are values-based organisations, committed to the seven cooperative principles.

Learn more about the seven co-operative principles directly from Co-operatives UK.
